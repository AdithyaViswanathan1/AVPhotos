Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our photography services

Booking & Scheduling

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and popular dates. For large events or peak seasons (holidays, graduation season), booking 1-2 months ahead ensures your preferred date and time are available.
We understand that plans can change. If you need to cancel or reschedule, please contact us at least 48 hours before your scheduled session. We'll work with you to find an alternative date that works for everyone.
Yes! While we're based in the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex, we're happy to travel to surrounding areas. Additional travel fees may apply for locations outside our primary service area. Contact us to discuss your specific location.

Services & Packages

We specialize in family portraits and event photography, including family reunions, birthday celebrations, graduations, cultural events, anniversary parties, and other special occasions. Each session is tailored to capture the unique moments and emotions of your event.
Our packages typically include professional photography coverage for the specified duration, professional editing of all images, high-resolution digital files delivered via online gallery, and printing rights. Specific package details and pricing are provided during consultation.
Absolutely! We have extensive experience photographing large family gatherings and events. We can capture group shots of everyone together as well as individual family units. Just let us know your needs when booking.

Photo Delivery & Rights

Edited photos are typically delivered within 2-3 weeks after your session or event. You'll receive a link to a private online gallery where you can view, download, and share your high-resolution images. Rush delivery may be available upon request.
You'll receive all professionally edited photos from your session. We carefully curate and edit each image to ensure the highest quality. The exact number varies depending on the session length and type of event, but you can expect a comprehensive collection of your special moments.
Yes! You receive full printing rights with your images. Feel free to print them at your preferred lab, share them on social media, create albums, or use them however you'd like. We only ask that you credit AV Photos when sharing publicly.
We primarily deliver high-resolution digital files that you can print anywhere. However, we're happy to provide recommendations for professional printing services and can discuss album options if you're interested. Contact us for more details.

Preparation & Day-Of

We recommend coordinating colors rather than matching exactly. Choose comfortable outfits in complementary colors that reflect your family's personality. Avoid busy patterns or logos that can be distracting. We're happy to provide specific guidance based on your session location and style preferences.
For outdoor sessions, we monitor weather forecasts closely. If severe weather is expected, we'll contact you to reschedule. Light clouds can actually create beautiful, soft lighting! We'll work together to find the best solution for your session.
