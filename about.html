About AV Photos

Capturing life's precious moments with artistry and heart

Welcome to AV Photos

Hello and Namaskar! My name is Adithya Viswanathan. I am a 24-year-old software developer at an AI startup and freelance photographer in the DFW area.

I was first introduced to photography at the age of 10 when my dad bought a point-and-shoot camera and let me try it out. I loved capturing beautiful moments and being able to look back on them in the future. So how can I stay idle when I know I can provide that same experience for others?

My experience lies in photographing graduations, families, birthdays and poojas! I strive to provide the clearest, simplest, and most vibrant images to my clients!

Photography Style

I believe in natural, timeless photography that captures real emotions and genuine connections. My approach combines classical composition with a modern aesthetic, resulting in elegant images that will stand the test of time.

What I Offer

Family Portraits: Beautiful, candid family photos that capture your family's unique dynamic and love for each other.

Event Photography: Comprehensive coverage of your special events, from birthdays and graduations to cultural celebrations and gatherings.

Professional Quality: All images are professionally edited and delivered in high resolution, ready for printing or sharing.

Serving the DFW Area

Based in the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex, I proudly serve families and clients throughout North Texas. From intimate home sessions to large event venues, I'm equipped to handle photography needs of all sizes.

Service Area: Dallas, Fort Worth, Arlington, Plano, Irving, Frisco, McKinney, and surrounding communities.
